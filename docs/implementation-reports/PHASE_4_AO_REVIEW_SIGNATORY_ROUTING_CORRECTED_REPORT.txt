PHASE 4 CORRECTED: AO REVIEW, SIGNATORY CHOICE AND ROUTING

This phase corrects the internal unit workflow after Chief Clerk drafting.

Implemented workflow:
1. Chief Clerk drafts and forwards to AO.
2. AO reviews the draft.
3. AO can return/disapprove with a reason.
4. If AO is the signatory, AO now signs/endorses internally and forwards to Commander.
5. If AO is not the signatory, AO selects a unit signatory officer and forwards the signal.
6. Selected signatory signs/endorses and forwards to Commander.
7. Commander remains the mandatory final approver before external Sign & Release.

Key corrections:
- The previous AO button text was misleading: "AO Approve -> Commander". It now reads "AO Sign/Endorse -> Commander".
- Backend action now treats AO direct-to-Commander route as AO signing/endorsement, not just approval.
- AO direct signing now sets unit_signatory_id to the AO and records signatory_signed_at.
- Commander account is required before the AO/signatory route can forward for final approval.
- Return/disapproval remains controlled with a required reason.
- Existing final digital Sign & Release remains after Commander approval only.
